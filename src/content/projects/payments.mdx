## 01 · The principle

> A payment system is measured by what happens when it fails, not when it works.

Every workflow I own is built around three rules: **idempotent on retry**, **observable in real time**, **reversible within one operator step**.

## 02 · What's inside

- Request → queue → adapter pattern so bank outages become back-pressure, not data loss.
- IDFC batch-ID generation with a deterministic seed and duplicate-detection table.
- SBI workflow hardening: retries with exponential backoff and a poison-letter queue.
- Reconciliation job that cross-checks our ledger against the bank statement every morning.
- IFSC validator with caching, TTL, and weekly refresh from the authoritative source.
